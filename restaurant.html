We Serve Food, Harmony, and Joy

Our Specialties

We use the best and most local ingredients. We are also proud to use meat reared on the lush pastures of our own farm in nearby Great Easton. Our chefs love working with farmers and estate owners – and can be sometimes be spotted foraging in the countryside for items such as berries, mushrooms and wild garlic. We believe quality ingredients, prepared with love and care, should be accessible to all, so our menu has options at a range of price points. This makes us the perfect venue whether you want to pop in one lunchtime for a ‘nibble’ in the bar or make an evening of it in

Supper Club

Our Supper Club takes places every Monday, offering two courses at one 7pm sitting. It’s hugely popular with locals (but everyone is welcome!) and the cost is only £15 per person for two courses. A typical main course might be beef-shin pappardelle or dukkah-spiced aubergine, while the dessert could be a vanilla parfait and mango coulis or a warm ginger pud with ice-cream. Call 01858 565288 or check our socials for this week’s menu and to book a table.
