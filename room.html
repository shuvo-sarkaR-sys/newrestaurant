Hotel Rooms

Room Details

We have 10 boutique bedrooms – six in the original stone building of the pub, and four in the old stable block above our beautiful courtyard. All have been recently refurbished, so combine a contemporary, chic quality with original character.

You’ll find such features as mullion windows, handpainted wallpaper, antiques and modern pieces, Hypnos mattresses, goose down bedding, beautiful artwork, roll-top baths and large walk-in showers. We also have a family room – complete with bunk beds – so are the perfect place for a family getaway.
